What is SFVM?

SFVM is a non-profit project focused on promoting and supporting the local music and arts scene in the San Fernando Valley. Our goal is to help grow a community of musicians, artists, and art lovers alike and to enable them to get out there and host their own local events.

 

SFVM relies on community involvement to stay afloat. You can support us by submitting events or by spreading the word and encouraging others to get involved.

 

You can follow us on instagram at @sfvm.la
