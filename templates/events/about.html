What is SFVM?

SFVM is a non-profit project focused on promoting and supporting local music and arts in the San Fernando Valley. We believe there is a lot of talent in the valley which is largely over looked -ignored in favor of traveling south into Los Angeles for something to do. We are here to change that.

 

SFVM relies on community involvement to stay afloat. You can support us by submitting events or by spreading the word and encouraging others to get involved.
